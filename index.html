Capentory is a system that allows our school to perform an inventory through a single platform. The system consists of a server and an Android app that communicate through a JSON-REST-API. The user only needs to scan barcodes through the app.

"Capentory – Capture Inventory" was my diploma project that I worked on with my three-man team with myself serving as the project leader and developer of the Android app.
